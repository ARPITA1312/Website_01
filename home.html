Our Founder

Aman Dhattarwal

Aman Dhattarwal was born on Tuesday, 4 March 1997 in Rajasthan, India. Aman attended Delhi Public School in Vasant Kunj and Delhi Public School in R.K. Puram, New Delhi. He completed a Bachelor of Engineering degree in Information Technology at Netaji Subhas Institute of Technology, New Delhi.

Aman Dhattarwal is an Indian-based teacher, YouTuber, entrepreneur, public speaker & social media personality. He is popular for his YouTube channel where he uploads educational & motivational videos. The channel includes all types of educational content including class 12, JEE, and much more.
